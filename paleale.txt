# Pale Ale
American-style hoppy pale ale.  Clean and tasty with citrus hop notes.


## Ingredients
6.6lbs Liquid Gold/Pale Malt Extract
2oz Cascade Hops (Pellets)
1oz Centennial Hops (Bittering)
1oz Summit (Aroma/Dry Hop)
Yeast (1056 Wyeast American Ale works well)
5 Gal Water

## Directions
You know what to do.  Dry hop the summits...
